# Privacy Policy — Super Calculadora

**Last updated: May 2026**

## Overview

Super Calculadora is a free, offline mathematical calculator. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

Super Calculadora operates entirely on your device. No information is sent to any server, third party, or cloud service.

## Data Stored Locally

The app stores the following data **only on your device**, using Android's SharedPreferences:

| Data | Purpose | Where stored |
|------|---------|--------------|
| Calculation history | Display recent operations | Device local storage |
| App settings (theme, notation preference, language) | Remember your preferences | Device local storage |

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

## Internet Access

Super Calculadora **does not require internet access** and does not make any network requests.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with:
- Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting services
- Social media SDKs

## Children's Privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Permissions

The app requests **no special Android permissions**. It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other device feature beyond local storage.

## Changes to This Policy

If the app ever changes to collect data, this policy will be updated and users will be notified via an app update.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/atiweb/super_calculadora).

*This app is open source. You can verify all data handling by reading the source code.*
