Privacy Policy

App: Super Calculadora  |  Last updated: May 2026  |  No data collected

Overview

Super Calculadora is a free, offline mathematical calculator. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.
The app operates entirely on your device. No information is ever sent to any server, third party, or cloud service.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

DataPurposeLocation
Calculation historyDisplay recent operationsDevice local storage only
App settings (theme, notation, language)Remember preferencesDevice local storage only

This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

Internet Access

Super Calculadora does not require internet access and makes no network requests of any kind.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or social media services of any kind.

Permissions

The app requests no special Android permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any device feature beyond local storage.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If the app ever changes to collect data, this policy will be updated and users will be notified via an app update.

Contact

For questions, open an issue on the GitHub repository.

Super Calculadora is open source — you can verify all data handling by reading the source code.
